### 🏡 First Section: Introduction to the Visitor System

The Visitor System in Home Alone Anomaly is a unique mechanic that allows players to adopt and care for visitors. These visitors can be any number of characters, from friendly neighbors to potential dangers. The goal is to keep your visitors happy and entertained so that they will stay longer and earn you more money.

#### 🌟 Key Points
- Visitors can be adopted from various sources, such as your home or the neighborhood.
- Players must provide basic needs such as food, water, and entertainment to keep visitors happy.
- Visitors can perform various tasks, such as cleaning up after themselves or providing security.

### 🛠️ Second Section: Adopting and Caring for Visitors

Adopting and caring for visitors is a crucial part of the Visitor System. Players must ensure that their visitors are happy and comfortable to earn the most money. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

#### 📝 Steps
1. **Find Visitors**: Look for visitors in your home or the neighborhood. They will be indicated by a star or other marker.
2. **Adopt Visitors**: Click on a visitor to adopt them. You will be prompted to name them and choose a type.
3. **Provide Needs**: Make sure to provide your visitors with food, water, and entertainment. They will be indicated by icons in their profile.
4. **Monitor Visitors**: Keep an eye on your visitors to ensure they are happy. If they are unhappy, try to find out what's wrong and fix it.

### 🚧 Third Section: Dealing with Potential Dangers

While visitors can be helpful, they can also pose dangers. It's important to be prepared to deal with potential dangers before they become a problem.

#### 🔔 Tips
- Keep an eye on your visitors' mood indicators. If they are angry or sad, they may be in danger.
- Use cameras to keep an eye on your visitors when you can't be there in person.
- Have a plan in place for dealing with emergencies, such as a visitor getting into a fight or causing a fire.

Once you select a class in Home Alone Anomaly, the decision is permanent. However, you can create a new character if you want to explore a different class.

#### Can I play as a Mage but use a sword?
Yes, but it won't be as effective as choosing a class that complements that playstyle, like Knight or Rogue.

Mage is ideal for solo play due to its powerful spells and ranged attacks. However, Rogue also works well if you want more mobility and hybrid play.
