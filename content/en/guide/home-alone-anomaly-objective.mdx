Home Alone Anomaly is a thrilling game that puts you in the shoes of a lone hero facing various challenges and enemies. The game requires strategic planning and resource management to succeed. In this guide, we'll cover the essential aspects of the game to help you win every time.

### 🛡️ FAQ

**Q: How do I upgrade my security system?**
A: To upgrade your security system, you need to gather resources and spend them at the upgrade menu. Prioritize upgrading cameras, motion sensors, and the alarm system for maximum security.

**Q: What is the best tool for gathering resources?**
A: The best tool for gathering resources depends on the type of resource you need. The axe is ideal for gathering wood, while the pickaxe is perfect for gathering stone.

**Q: How do I complete side quests?**
A: To complete side quests, you need to follow the instructions provided by the quest giver. Some quests may require you to defeat enemies, gather resources, or complete specific tasks.
