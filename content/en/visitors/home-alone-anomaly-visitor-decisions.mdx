## Introduction

In "Home Alone Anomaly," you are left alone in your home while managing various visitors with different intentions. Making the right decisions is crucial to ensure your safety and the successful completion of your tasks. This guide will walk you through the process of evaluating each visitor and making informed decisions.

## Evaluating Visitors

### Trustworthy Visitors

Trustworthy visitors are those who help you with your chores and contribute to the cleanliness and order of your home. These visitors can be identified by their cooperative behavior and helpful attitude.

**Examples of Trustworthy Visitors**:
- Those who assist you with cleaning tasks
- Visitors who help you organize items
- Individuals who offer to help with chores

### Untrustworthy Visitors

Untrustworthy visitors are those who have malicious intentions or disrupt your routine. These visitors can be identified by their suspicious behavior and attempts to interfere with your tasks.

**Examples of Untrustworthy Visitors**:
- Visitors who try to steal items or cause chaos
- Individuals who attempt to enter your home without permission
- People who offer gifts or promises in exchange for personal information

## Making Decisions

When evaluating visitors, consider the following factors to make informed decisions:

- **Visitor's Behavior**: Observe their actions and interactions with you and your surroundings.
- **Visitor's Intentions**: Assess whether their actions align with helping you or causing harm.
- **Safety of Your Home**: Prioritize the security and integrity of your home.
- **Completion of Tasks**: Ensure that your tasks are being completed efficiently and effectively.

## Examples of Decision-Making Scenarios

### Scenario 1: A Visitor Offers to Help Clean

If a visitor offers to help clean, evaluate their behavior and ensure they are not causing any disruptions. If they are cooperative and helpful, you can trust them to assist you with the cleaning tasks.

### Scenario 2: A Visitor Requests Personal Information

If a visitor requests personal information, be cautious. Do not provide any sensitive information and be wary of their intentions. If they are trying to manipulate you or steal your belongings, it's best to decline their request and keep a close eye on them.

### Scenario 3: A Visitor Attempts to Enter Without Permission

If a visitor attempts to enter your home without permission, be firm in your decision. Do not allow them to enter and inform them that you are not comfortable with their presence. If necessary, call for help or lock yourself in a safe room.

## Conclusion

Making the right decisions in "Home Alone Anomaly" is essential to ensure your safety and the successful completion of your tasks. By evaluating each visitor's behavior and intentions, you can navigate through the chaos and emerge victorious. Remember to stay cautious, prioritize the safety of your home, and make informed decisions to progress in the game.
