### Introduction to Home Alone Anomaly

Home Alone Anomaly is a thrilling game where you play as a child left alone in their home while their parents are away. Your goal is to earn money by completing chores and avoiding the various characters that will attempt to enter your home. This guide will provide you with the essential information and strategies to help you survive the night and earn the most money.

### Understanding the Gameplay

#### Home Alone Anomaly Gameplay Mechanics

Home Alone Anomaly is a survival game where you must complete a series of chores to earn money. The game features a variety of characters, including a vampire, a maintenance worker, and a lost child, each with their own unique challenges. You must be careful to lock your doors, check your cameras, and be on the lookout for any signs of trouble.

#### How to Earn Money

To earn money, you must complete chores such as cleaning, cooking, and taking care of the plants. Each chore has a specific amount of money associated with it, and you must be quick and efficient to earn the most money possible.

#### Avoiding Troublesome Characters

One of the key challenges in Home Alone Anomaly is avoiding the various characters that will attempt to enter your home. Each character has their own motivations and behaviors, and you must be vigilant to prevent them from causing trouble.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Surviving the Night

#### Initial Setup

1. **Lock Your Doors**: Before anything else, make sure all your doors are locked. This will prevent the characters from entering your home.
2. **Check Your Cameras**: Place cameras in strategic locations around your home to keep an eye on any potential intruders.
3. **Prepare Your Chores**: Make a list of all the chores you need to complete and plan your strategy accordingly.

#### Core Progression

1. **Complete Chores**: Begin by completing the chores that earn the most money first. Be sure to check your cameras and listen for any signs of trouble while you work.
2. **Manage Resources**: Keep an eye on your inventory and resources. Make sure you have everything you need to complete each chore.

#### Advanced Tactics

1. **Use the Right Tools**: Some chores require specific tools, so make sure you have them on hand before you start.
2. **Be Prepared for Unexpected Visitors**: Keep a close eye on your cameras and be ready to respond quickly if a character attempts to enter your home.
